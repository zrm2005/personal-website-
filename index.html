Welcome

Welcome to my website. My name is Zach Molnar, and I am currently studying Information Technology at the University of Missouri. This website shares information about my background, interests, and growth as a student preparing for a career in technology.

I am especially interested in web development, problem solving, and learning how technology can improve the way businesses operate. Through my coursework and hands-on experience, I have started building skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, databases, and general IT support.

One of my most valuable professional experiences was working as an IT and Web Development Intern at EDSI Engineering. During that internship, I helped rebuild and redesign the company website while communicating with leadership and different departments to make sure the final product matched company needs.

This site is designed to grow over time as I continue building projects, learning new skills, and gaining experience. It serves as a place where I can present my work in a clear and professional way while practicing the web design concepts I am learning in class.
